  • What is the process for ordering a custom web design and development service?
    Custom order:To order a custom web design and development service, simply contact us through our website or email and provide us with your project requirements. Our team will work with you to understand your needs and provide you with a quote. If you decide to move forward with the project, we'll send you a contract and request an initial deposit payment. We'll then send you a brief questionnaire to better understand your requirements and preferences, and our team will get to work on creating your website.
    Standard order order:To order standard web design for blogs, portfolios and for basic needs, simply go to the “Packages” section and purchase a package that best fits your requirements and budget. After purchasing a package, our team will then send you a brief questionnaire to better understand your requirements and preferences, and our team will get to work on creating your website.
    What is included in the cost of a custom web design and development service?
    The cost of a custom web design and development service includes all agreed-upon design and development work, as well as any revisions or changes requested during the project.
    How much do you charge for a custom web design and development service?
    Our pricing varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. Please contact us for a quote and we'll be happy to provide you with an estimate.
    Do you require a deposit payment for custom web design?
    Yes, we require an initial deposit payment to start work on your project. The deposit amount is typically 20% of the total project cost.
    When do I pay the remaining balance?
    The remaining balance is due upon completion of the project. We'll send you an invoice for the remaining balance, and once it's paid, we'll launch your website.
    What if I'm not satisfied with the final product?
    We strive to ensure that all of our clients are completely satisfied with their final product. If you're not happy with the final result, please let us know and we'll work with you to make any necessary revisions or changes.
    What is your turnaround time for a custom web design and development service?
    Our turnaround time varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. We'll provide you with an estimated timeframe when we provide you with a quote.
